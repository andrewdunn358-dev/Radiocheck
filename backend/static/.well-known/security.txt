# Radio Check — Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in Radio Check, please report it responsibly.

**Contact**: security@radiocheck.me
**PGP Key**: Available on request

## What to Report

- Authentication or authorization bypass
- Data exposure (PII, credentials, session tokens)
- Cross-site scripting (XSS) or injection vulnerabilities
- Server-side request forgery (SSRF)
- AI safety system bypass or manipulation
- Safeguarding system failures

## What NOT to Report

- Automated scanner output without manual verification
- Social engineering
- Denial of service
- Issues in third-party services we don't control

## Our Commitment

- We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- We will provide an initial assessment within 5 working days
- We will not take legal action against researchers acting in good faith
- We will credit researchers (with permission) when a fix is released

## Scope

- `radiocheck.me` and all subdomains
- The Radio Check mobile application
- API endpoints at `api.radiocheck.me`

## Safe Harbour

We consider security research conducted in accordance with this policy to be:
- Authorized under the Computer Misuse Act 1990
- Exempt from DMCA restrictions
- Conducted in good faith

Thank you for helping keep our veteran community safe.
