# Radio Check - Business & Funding Plan

## Executive Summary

**Radio Check** is a digital mental health and peer support platform specifically designed for UK military veterans. The platform addresses the critical gap in accessible, veteran-centric mental health support by combining AI-powered therapeutic conversations with human peer support and professional counselling services.

### The Opportunity
- **2.4 million** UK veterans, with 1 in 5 experiencing mental health challenges
- **72% of veterans** prefer peer support from those with shared military experience
- **Stigma barrier**: Traditional mental health services see only 30% engagement from veterans
- **Digital gap**: No UK-focused, comprehensive veteran mental health app exists

### Our Solution
A mobile-first platform offering:
- 24/7 AI therapeutic companions with military-aware training
- Live chat with trained peer supporters and counsellors
- Robust safeguarding with real-time risk detection
- Callback request system for flexible support
- Staff management portal for organisations
