1. Overview

Radio Check is a comprehensive mental health and peer support platform designed specifically for UK military veterans. As an administrator, you have full access to manage staff, content, and monitor all platform activity.

Key Responsibilities

  • Managing counsellor and peer supporter accounts
  • Creating and scheduling staff rotas
  • Managing app content through the CMS
  • Monitoring safeguarding alerts and escalations
  • Reviewing analytics and generating reports
  • Managing AI character configurations
  • Ensuring clinical governance compliance

2. Getting Started

Logging In

  1. Navigate to the Admin Dashboard URL
  2. Enter your admin email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Login"
Tip

If you've forgotten your password, contact the system administrator for a reset.

Dashboard Layout

The admin dashboard is organised into tabs across the top:

  • Staff - Manage counsellors, peers, and supervisors
  • Rota - Create and manage shift schedules
  • CMS - Edit app content and resources
  • Analytics - View usage statistics and reports
  • Safeguarding - Monitor alerts and escalations
  • Organisations - Manage support organisation directory
  • AI Characters - Configure AI chat personas
  • Governance - Clinical safety and compliance

3. Staff Management

User Roles

Radio Check has four user roles with different permission levels:

  • Admin - Full system access (you)
  • Supervisor - Team management, escalation handling, supervision notes
  • Counsellor - Clinical support, case management, live chat
  • Peer Supporter - Peer support, can escalate to supervisors

Creating a New Staff Member

  1. Go to the Staff tab
  2. Click + Add Staff
  3. Fill in the required details:
    • Name
    • Email (used for login)
    • Role (Admin/Supervisor/Counsellor/Peer)
    • Phone number
    • Specialisation (for counsellors)
  4. Click Save
Important

New staff members will receive an email with their temporary password. They should change this on first login.

Editing Staff Details

Click the Edit button next to any staff member to update their details or change their role.

Deactivating Staff

To deactivate a staff member (e.g., when they leave), click Deactivate. This preserves their records but prevents login.

4. Rota & Scheduling

Creating Shifts

  1. Go to the Rota tab
  2. Click on a date in the calendar
  3. Select Add Shift
  4. Choose the staff member
  5. Set start and end times
  6. Add any notes (optional)
  7. Click Save

Shift Coverage

The rota shows coverage status:

  • Green - Adequate coverage
  • Amber - Limited coverage
  • Red - No coverage
Best Practice

Aim for at least one counsellor and one peer supporter on each shift to ensure appropriate support coverage.

5. Content Management (CMS)

Editable Content Areas

  • Home Page - Welcome message, featured content
  • Menu Items - App navigation cards
  • Resources - Support articles and guides
  • Crisis Support - Emergency contact information
  • Podcasts - Recommended podcast list

Editing Content

  1. Go to the CMS tab
  2. Select the content section to edit
  3. Make your changes in the editor
  4. Click Preview to see how it will appear
  5. Click Publish to make changes live
Note

Content changes go live immediately after publishing. Consider previewing on a test device first.

Generating Reports

  1. Go to the Reports section

7. Safeguarding

Alert Types

  • High Risk - Immediate attention required (suicidal ideation, self-harm)
  • Medium Risk - Requires follow-up within 24 hours
  • Low Risk - Monitor and review
Critical

High-risk safeguarding alerts must be actioned immediately. If geolocation is available, emergency services can be contacted.

Responding to Alerts

  1. Review the alert details and chat history
  2. Assess the risk level
  3. Take appropriate action (contact user, escalate, contact emergency services)
  4. Document the response
  5. Mark as resolved with outcome notes

Escalation Workflow

Staff can escalate concerns to supervisors. As admin, you can view all escalations and ensure they are handled appropriately.

8. Live Chat Monitoring

Monitoring Chats

The Chat History tab shows all live chat sessions. You can:

  • View active and completed chats
  • Read chat transcripts
  • See which staff member handled each chat
  • Review chat duration and outcomes
Privacy Note

Chat messages are encrypted at rest. Only authorised administrators can view chat content for quality assurance purposes.

11. Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Can't log in

  • Check email and password are correct
  • Ensure Caps Lock is off
  • Try clearing browser cache
  • Contact system administrator for password reset

Dashboard not loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Try a different browser
  • Clear browser cache and cookies

Data not updating

  • Click the refresh button on the relevant section
  • Wait a few moments and try again
  • Check if other admin functions are working

Getting Help

For technical support, contact:

  • Email: support@radiocheck.me
  • Emergency technical issues: Contact system administrator
